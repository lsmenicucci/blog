Stochastic Series Expansion: General Framework

We seek to establish the basic mathematical grounding for simulating thermodynamic quantities of many-body quantum systems. Specifically, we aim to implement a program that can simulate spin-1/2 systems. For accomplishing that task, I’ve chooses to work with the stochastic series expansion framework which will end up being implemented on top of a metropolis algorithm together with loop updates1. The developed content was produced from my notes when studding the amazing “Computational Studies of Quantum Spin Systems” from Sandvik2 which were a fantastic guide to this topic.
